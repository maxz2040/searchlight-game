### How to Play

Click on the screen when you locate the objects hidden in each scene. At the end of each scene use a flashlight or magnifying glass to search for clues.

- **Starting the Game**
  - To begin, click on the arrow button in the magnifying glass on the welcome screen.
    - This will play a short cut scene explaining the premise of the story. You can skip this intro by clicking on the gold and brown button in the lower right.
    - In the first level, a drawing will point out things you should look for from the crime scene. This serves as a tutorial.
  - The welcome screen also has buttons for controlling sound and developer information.
  - Your web browser automatically stores your high score and game progress.
- **Game Controls**
  - Desktop
    - For the finding minigame, click on the matching items with a mouse.
    - For the clue minigame, use your mouse to identify the location of the photo fragments.
    - For the final minigame, click on and drag the fragments using the mouse.
- **Game Play**

  - This game consists of three different minigames. Two of the minigames make up the first three levels of the game. The final minigame comprises the fourth and final level of the game.
  - The game gives you the option to replay a completed level to increase your high score.
  - The culprit will manifest very briefly in each of the levels.
  - The game will give you a free hint for the first two minigames at the first level.

  - The first minigame involves searching for specific objects in a messy room.
    - You have a brief period to look for the specific objects listed in the bottom row.
    - The longer it takes for you to find the objects, the lower your star rating will be.
    - For every two items you find in succession, you gain an extra five seconds of time.
    - If you’re stumped, you can ask for hints from the game by clicking on the hints button in the lower right corner.
      - In each level, you get three hints.
      - Taking hints will reduce the time you have left and will reduce your final score.
  - The second minigame involves looking for clues in the now-cleared room. You must find all three photo fragments before the time is up.
    - Fred’s version uses a flashlight, Daphne’s a magnifying glass, and Velma’s a scanner. They all function the same.
    - Move the mouse to navigate through the room.
    - Once you find a fragment, click on it.
    - You have a much shorter time frame to look for the fragments.

  - The last minigame is a jigsaw puzzle.

    - Assemble the photo pieces you’ve found to learn the identity of the culprit.

    - Once you find the identity of the ghost, choose from the three suspects in the house.
    - If you chose the right culprit, you’ve won the game.
- **Gameplay Hints & Strategies**
  - Don’t overlook the obvious when searching for items. Sometimes, the items are exactly where you expect them to be.
  - Look for some items where they would be inconspicuous. A leaf or an eye pattern may disappear into a painting without you noticing.
  - You only have a limited number of hints per level. Because of this and their time penalty, use them only when you need to.
  - You can only play the levels in order. Once you do finish a level, you can choose to replay it.
- **Audience**
  - Fans of the Scooby Doo franchise both young and old will love this find the object game.
  - While this game has no adult content, it does have jump scares. This might take younger children by surprise.
